Portugal

About Portugal

Capital: Lisbon


Official language: Portuguese


With its mild climate, 3000 hours of sunshine per year and 850 kms of splendid beaches bathed by the Atlantic Ocean, Portugal is the perfect holiday destination all year round. This is a country that has the oldest borders in Europe, with an exceptional range of different landscapes just a short distance away, lots of leisure activities and a unique cultural heritage, where tradition and modernity blend together in perfect harmony. Its superb cuisine, fine wines and hospitable people make this a tourist paradise of the highest quality.

My trip to Portugal

Travel length: 3 weeks, July 2022


Cities I travelled to: Lisbon, Sintra, Estoril, Cascais, Algarve

Attractions

Cabo da Roca

Visit the westernmost point of continental Europe. Enjoy breathtaking cliffs and sea views.

National Palace of Pena

Explore a fairy-tale castle on top of the Sintra mountains with colorful architecture.

Old Houses

Wander through charming old houses and narrow streets in historic neighborhoods.

Lagos, Algarve

Relax on the stunning beaches of Lagos and explore the beautiful Algarve region.

Cuisines

Pastel de Nata

Indulge in the famous Portuguese custard tart pastry, a delightful sweet treat.

Paella

Savor the flavors of Portuguese paella, a delicious seafood and rice dish.

Salt Cod Croquettes

Try the savory and crispy salt cod croquettes, a popular Portuguese appetizer.

Homemade Caldo verde

Warm up with a bowl of homemade Caldo verde, a traditional Portuguese green soup.
